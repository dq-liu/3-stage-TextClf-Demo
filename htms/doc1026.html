The holidays might be over , but awards season is officially here . ‘ Dead Like Me ’ When to watch : Now , on Hulu and Amazon . For a show that debuted over 15 years ago , “ Dead Like Me ” doesn ’ t feel crusty or dated . If anything , it still feels creative and fresh , poignant and memorable .
