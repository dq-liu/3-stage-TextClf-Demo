Alisa Ann Friscia and Kevin Christopher Guidette are to be married Feb. 17 at Stella Maris Roman Catholic Church in San Juan , P.R . The Rev . Her mother retired as a Spanish teacher at Livingston High School in Livingston , N.J . The groom , 29 , is a new business sales executive at Dow Jones & Company in Manhattan . He graduated from the University of Connecticut . He is the son of Mary Regina Guidette and Christopher Lino Guidette of Otis , Mass . The groom ’ s mother retired as the superintendent of schools for Middlesex County , N.J.
