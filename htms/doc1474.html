Stephen Colbert broadcast live on Tuesday , responding to President Trump ’ s State of the Union address with jokes written just moments before . Trump ’ s delivery was , by the president ’ s standards , restrained and uneventful . But Colbert gave it no high marks . “ This was not a particularly good speech , but what it lacked in quality it made up for in length . This speech was like watching paint lie. ” — STEPHEN COLBERT Exaggerating the relative strength of the United States economy , Trump told Congress , “ We are considered far and away the hottest economy anywhere in the world. ” That put Colbert in mind of one of Trump ’ s more infamous moments , from 2006 . “ It is so hot , if it wasn ’ t my economy perhaps I ’ d be dating it. ” — STEPHEN COLBERT , referring to a statement the president made on “ The View ” about his daughter Ivanka And Colbert joked about Speaker Nancy Pelosi ’ s largely unenthusiastic reactions to Trump ’ s speech . “ She showed great restraint , did not stick out her tongue once .
