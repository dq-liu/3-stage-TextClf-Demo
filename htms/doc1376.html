Mary Beth Barone , Shelby Slauer and Megan Patsel put together this show to honor Mike Turzilli , their good friend and a comedian , who died in 2017 from an overdose of opioids . What if one man experienced all of human history ? What if that man were the comedian and musician Dave Hill ? For this new live show , Hill has teamed up with Jim Biederman , executive producer of “ Join or Die With Craig Ferguson ” ; Jodi Lennon , a writer for “ At Home With Amy Sedaris ” ; and Hill ’ s podcast producer , Chris Gersbeck . The series takes up residency in Astoria , Queens , throughout February and March , and places Hill at the center of various historical moments , with his producers asking him how he got there . 347-451-3873 , qedastoria.com
