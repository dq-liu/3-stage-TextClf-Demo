Most wedding day survival kits are equipped with an arsenal of essential items to help brides avoid any mishaps or unexpected hiccups . Double-sided tape , bobby pins and a bevy of pills , ranging from aspirin to allergy medication , can certainly prove useful . But here are some additional products to help you get ahead of any potential stressor — whether a pesky facial breakout or an overbearing relative . The Heart Chakra candle features a blend of the fragrant wood palo santo and rose , which creates a calming , heart-opening experience that helps you to focus on your love and union .
